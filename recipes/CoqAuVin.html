What is Coq Au Vin exactly?

Hailing from France, this wonderful chicken stew with drumsticks and chicken thighs is braised in a delicious wine sauce and then baked to perfection! And, to top it off, this dish is also filled with bacon, mushrooms, and carrots

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Drumsticks
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Onion
  • carrots
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chicken Broth
  • Red Wine
  1. Preheat oven and cook bacon until it crumbles
  2. Sear the chicken
  3. Add the vegetables
  4. Make the sauce and braise
  5. Take out the chicken
  6. Thicken the sauce
  7. Simmer
